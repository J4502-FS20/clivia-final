Data of International students at MU

Mizzou was home to approximately 1,814 international students.
Mizzou suggests that international students make up about 6.1% of the student body.
Chinese students could account for as much as 3.5% of the entire student body,
and as much as 58.0% of the international student body at Mizzou.
Over the past several years the total international population of students on campus has shrunk.
China was the largest contributor to this decline.
